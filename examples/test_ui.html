Audience Growth Rate & GTM Signal

We measure how fast your audience is growing month-over-month and benchmark it against other startups at your stage in your specific market. This is the primary proxy for early traction before revenue data exists.

📈 Two Compute Paths — System Picks Automatically
PATH A — PitchBook
If your startup is tracked in PitchBook, we pull webGrowthRate, socialGrowthRate, and percentile rankings directly from the API. Z-scores are computed via invNorm of your percentile vs your sector peers. No manual data needed.
PATH B — GA4 + Manual
If PitchBook has no data on you yet (common at early Pre-Seed), we pull sessions from GA4 Data API (OAuth) and use your manually entered social counts. Benchmarked against our static market table.
# Path B formulas
web_growth% = (sessions_curr - sessions_prev) ÷ sessions_prev × 100
AGR_raw = (web_growth% + social_growth%) ÷ 2 # or single channel if one missing
AudienceGrowthZ = (w_web × z_web) + (w_soc × z_social) # weights by market type

Early Engagement Velocity — Supplementary
Waitlist & beta user counts Optional supplementary metric
EEV measures how fast high-intent users engage relative to your total audience. 5% EEV is considered excellent at Seed stage. Leave blank if you have no waitlist or beta programme yet.
EEV Formula & what it means
EEV = ((Δ Waitlist + Δ Beta Users) ÷ Unique Visitors) × 100

This metric captures conversion intent — how many people who discover your product take a high-commitment action (join waitlist or enter beta). It's a leading indicator of product-market pull before you have revenue.
Example calculation:
Waitlist: May 600 → Jun 800 (Δ = +200)
Beta: May 100 → Jun 150 (Δ = +50)
Visitors Jun: 5,000
EEV = (200+50) ÷ 5000 × 100 = 5.0% ← Excellent

Patent Status — Bonus Points Only · No Penalty
Do you have a patent related to your product? Optional · additive only · zero penalty if none
Declaring a patent triggers automatic verification against three free government databases. A verified result adds bonus points to your base score. No match found — base score is unchanged. No declaration — no mention, no penalty.
Patent verification — what happens automatically
When you declare a patent, our system runs a 3-step async verification using free official US government APIs. It does not block your score — the base score is shown immediately. The patent bonus is added when verification completes.

New applications take 12–18 months to appear in public USPTO records. If yours isn't found, you'll see "Founder-declared, unverified" — this does NOT reduce your score. It simply means the record isn't public yet.

STEP 02 — Broader Discovery
PatentsView API
Searches by company name or inventor name across all US granted patents.
api.patentsview.org/patents/query?q={assignee_organization}
STEP 03 — Ownership Confirm
Patent Assignment Search
Confirms patent is assigned to your company (vs individual or holding entity).
assignment-api.uspto.gov/patent/search?assignee={company}
All three APIs are free, require no authentication, and are official US government sources. Verification runs asynchronously — your score is computed immediately; the patent bonus is added when the result arrives.
